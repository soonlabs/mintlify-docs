# Create an Agent

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the Console">
    Go to [nonprod.nebulaclaw.ai](https://nonprod.nebulaclaw.ai) and sign in with Google.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Agent Creation">
    If you don't have any agents yet, you'll see the **Create Agent** page automatically. Otherwise, navigate there from the sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Your Agent">
    - **Name** — Choose a descriptive name for your agent. This will be visible in the console.
    - **AI Model** — Select your preferred model:

    | Model | Best For |
    |-------|----------|
    | GPT-4o | High-quality responses, complex conversations |
    | GPT-4o-mini | Cost-effective, high-volume groups |

    <Info>GPT-4o is recommended for most use cases. Models marked with a "Recommended" badge offer the best balance of quality and cost.</Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for Provisioning">
    After clicking **Create**, your agent will begin provisioning. This typically takes 20-30 seconds.

    The console automatically polls for status updates — no need to refresh the page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Agent Ready">
    Once the status indicator turns green, your agent is online and ready to connect to a channel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>Agent names cannot be changed after creation. Choose carefully.</Warning>
