# Persona Configuration

The **Persona** defines the Bot's tone, speaking style, and behavior. NebulaClaw ships with four community role presets — and also supports fully custom personas.

## Four Community Presets

| Preset | Best for | Tone highlights |
|--------|----------|-----------------|
| **Project Community** | Web3 projects, product communities | Professional, patient, grounded in project docs + anti-shill |
| **KOL / Alpha Group** | Paid alpha / research groups | Neutral, high signal-to-noise, DYOR reminders, spam filtering |
| **Trader Community** | Trading discussion groups | Market-neutral, no investment advice, risk disclosures |
| **Meme Community** | Degen / meme-coin groups | High-energy, meme-aware, but strict on scam links and fake airdrops |

Each preset ships with a matching **avatar** and tuned persona prompt. Clicking a preset applies it instantly and the chat preview on the right updates live.

## Custom Persona

Select **Custom** to edit the persona prompt directly. Common dimensions to customize:

- How the Bot speaks (tone, formality, preferred language)
- Topics to emphasize or avoid
- Project-specific routing (e.g. "route staking questions to /staking")
- Specific role definition or background

**Example (appended on top of the "Project Community" preset):**

```
You are the official community manager for [ProjectName].
- For staking questions, route users to the /staking docs
- Do not discuss token price or give investment advice
- Proactively warn users about messages mentioning "guaranteed returns" or "cashback rewards"
- Maintain a professional, friendly tone
```

<Tip>Switching to a different preset **preserves your custom text** — compare presets freely and switch back to Custom when ready.</Tip>

## Persona vs. Knowledge Base — which goes where?

| Put in the Persona | Put in the Knowledge Base |
|-------------------|--------------------------|
| **How** to speak — tone, attitude, taboo topics | **What** to say — specific FAQs, product facts, rules |
| Constant across every conversation | Retrieved on demand |
| Keep short (shipped with every request) | Can hold many documents |

<Warning>A long persona prompt adds to **every** response's token cost. **Keep concrete knowledge in the knowledge base, not the persona.**</Warning>

## Saving Changes

The **Save** button appears at the bottom of the page when you have unsaved changes. Saves take effect immediately — no Bot restart required.
