# Pricing

NebulaClaw uses a **monthly subscription** model — simple and transparent.

## Core Pricing

<Card title="$9.99 / month" icon="bot">

  - 1 always-on 24/7 AI Bot
  - **$5.00 monthly token credits** (unused credits **reset to zero** at month-end)
  - Full feature access: Knowledge Q&A, smart moderation, analytics
  - All 7 AI models available (Economy / Balanced / Premium tiers)
  - Multi-group management, auto language detection
</Card>

## Token Credit Mechanics

AI inference is metered in tokens:

| Credit type | Source | Expiry |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| **Monthly credits** | $5.00 included with subscription | **Resets monthly** — unused balance does not carry over |
| **Top-up credits** | Buy packs from Billing console | **Never expire** — balance stays until consumed |

- Monthly credits are consumed first; once depleted, the bot automatically draws from top-up balance
- Top-up packs are priced by size — larger packs have better per-unit rates

<Tip>High-volume groups should keep a top-up pack on standby. When the monthly $5 runs out, the bot switches to top-up credits seamlessly — no interruption.</Tip>

## How Many Groups Can One Bot Serve?

Each account currently has **1 Bot**, but that **one Bot can serve multiple Telegram groups**:

- Add the same `@botUsername` to each group and promote it to admin
- Register each group's Chat ID in **Moderation → Telegram Groups**
- Per-group **Require Mention** and **Fallback Language** settings
- Moderation rules and knowledge base are Bot-level — configure once, apply everywhere

<Info>**Multi-Bot** (multiple independent Bots per account) is on the roadmap — useful for running distinct Bots per project / per community preset. In the meantime, a single Bot with multi-group management covers most use cases.</Info>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods are supported?">
    Payment is processed via Stripe — all major credit and debit cards accepted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    **No refunds.** All subscription payments and top-up packs are final — please confirm your needs before subscribing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you provide invoices?">
    **No formal invoices / tax receipts are issued at this time.** Payment history is visible under **Billing → History**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to unused monthly credits?">
    **They reset to zero at month-end** — monthly credits do not carry over. Top-up credits are unaffected.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I cancel?">
    You keep access until the end of the current billing period. After that the bot stops responding until you resubscribe. Top-up credits **are not lost** on cancellation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How many groups can one Bot serve?">
    No hard limit — the same `@botUsername` can be added to multiple groups. Register each Chat ID in **Moderation → Telegram Groups**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I create multiple independent Bots?">
    Each account currently has **1 Bot**. Multi-Bot support is on the roadmap.
  </Accordion>
