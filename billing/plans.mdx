# Plans & Pricing

NebulaClaw offers flexible plans to match your community's needs.

## Available Plans

| Feature | Free | Pro | Enterprise |
|---------|------|-----|-----------|
| AI Messages | Limited | Higher limit | Custom |
| AI Models | GPT-4o-mini | GPT-4o + mini | All models |
| Knowledge Docs | 5 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Moderation Rules | 3 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Analytics | Basic | Advanced | Advanced + Export |
| Support | Community | Priority | Dedicated |

## Upgrading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to **Billing** from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Plan">
    Click **Upgrade** and choose your desired plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Checkout">
    Enter payment details and confirm. Your plan activates immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>Plan changes take effect immediately. You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the billing period.</Note>

## Downgrading

You can downgrade at any time. The change takes effect at the end of your current billing period. Features above your new plan's limits become read-only until you reduce usage or re-upgrade.
