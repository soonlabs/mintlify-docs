# Analytics Dashboard

Track your agent's activity, community health, and moderation effectiveness from a single dashboard.

## Available Metrics

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Message Volume" icon="chart-line">
    Total messages processed over time, with daily/weekly trends.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active Users" icon="users">
    Unique users who interacted with or triggered the agent.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sentiment Analysis" icon="face-smile">
    Breakdown of message sentiment: positive, neutral, and negative.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Moderation Actions" icon="shield">
    Summary of mutes, kicks, and bans executed by auto-moderation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Filters

Customize the dashboard view with:

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| **Time Range** | 7 days, 14 days, 30 days |
| **Group** | All groups or a specific Telegram group |
| **Timezone** | Your local timezone for accurate daily breakdowns |

<Tip>Use sentiment trends to fine-tune your agent's personality. A drop in positive sentiment may indicate the agent's tone needs adjustment.</Tip>
