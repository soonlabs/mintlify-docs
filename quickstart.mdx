# Quickstart

Get your AI community agent up and running in five steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to the [NebulaClaw Console](https://nonprod.nebulaclaw.ai/login) and sign in with your Google account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your Agent">
    After signing in, click **Create Agent**. Give your agent a name and select an AI model:

    - **GPT-4o** — Best quality, recommended for most use cases
    - **GPT-4o-mini** — Faster and more cost-effective for high-volume groups

    <Note>Agent names cannot be changed after creation.</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Telegram">
    1. Open Telegram and search for **@BotFather**
    2. Send `/newbot` and follow the prompts to create a new bot
    3. Copy the bot token (looks like `123456:ABC-DEF1234ghIkl-zyx57W2v...`)
    4. Paste the token in the **Telegram Token** field on the Agent Overview page
    5. Click **Save**

    <Tip>Your agent will automatically connect to Telegram once the token is saved.</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Personality">
    Go to the **Personality** page and choose a preset:

    | Preset | Style |
    |--------|-------|
    | Friendly | Casual, uses emoji, approachable |
    | Professional | Formal, business-like tone |
    | Creative | Imaginative, playful responses |
    | Concise | Brief, to-the-point answers |

    Or select **Custom** and write your own personality description.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add to a Group">
    1. Add your bot to a Telegram group
    2. Promote it to **Admin** (required for moderation features)
    3. Send a message — your AI agent will respond!

    The group will automatically appear in **Moderation > Telegram Groups**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
