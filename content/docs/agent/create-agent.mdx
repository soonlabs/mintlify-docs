---

Bot creation is a **4-step wizard**: Subscribe → Preset → Model → Name. The whole flow takes about a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in and open the wizard">
    Go to [app.nebulaclaw.ai](https://app.nebulaclaw.ai) and sign in with Google. If you don't have any bots yet, you're redirected to the creation page; otherwise open **Bots → Create** from the sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Subscribe ($9.99/month)">
    Creating a Bot requires a monthly subscription:

    - **$9.99 / month** (auto-renews, cancel anytime)
    - **$5.00 monthly token credits** (resets at the end of each billing cycle)
    - Full feature access (knowledge base / moderation / analytics / all 7 models)

    Click **Get Started** to check out via Stripe. You're returned to the wizard on success.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a community preset">
    Choose the preset closest to your community:

    - **Project Community** — Web3 projects / product communities
    - **KOL / Alpha Group** — paid alpha / research groups
    - **Trader Community** — trading discussion groups
    - **Meme Community** — degen / meme-coin groups

    Each preset ships with a matching avatar and tuned persona prompt. You can switch or fully customize it later on the [Personality page](/agent/personality).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a model tier">
    Three tiers, seven models:

    | Tier | Example models | Best for |
    |------|---------------|----------|
    | **Economy** | `gpt-4.1-nano`, `gpt-4o-mini` | High volume, cost-sensitive |
    | **Balanced** (default) | `gpt-5-mini`, `gpt-4.1-mini` | General use |
    | **Premium** | `gpt-5.2`, `gpt-4o`, `gpt-4.1` | Demanding Q&A, complex moderation |

    <Info>Default is **Balanced / `gpt-5-mini`**. Both tier and specific model are switchable in Agent settings later.</Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Name the Bot">
    Enter a slug (lowercase letters, numbers, hyphens — max 63 chars), e.g. `my-project-bot`.

    Click **Create Bot**. The platform assigns a pre-registered Telegram Bot from its Bot Pool and starts the Agent gateway — ready in about 30 seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Bot is ready">
    When the status turns green, the Bot is online. The console shows its `@botUsername`; click **Open in Telegram** to jump straight to a private chat.

    Next, [add it to your Telegram group](/channels/telegram).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>**A Bot's avatar and username cannot be changed after creation.** Pick the name and preset carefully.</Warning>
